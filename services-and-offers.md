## How should I choose between wall-mounted, freestanding, and mobile lockers?

Wall-mounted lockers are often considered when wall space is available and floor space needs to remain open. Freestanding lockers are useful when storage needs to be placed away from walls or in flexible room layouts. Mobile lockers are useful when equipment storage needs to move, support cleaning access, or adapt to multi-use spaces.
