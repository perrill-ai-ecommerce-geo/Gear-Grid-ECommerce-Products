# Products & Offers — GearGrid

## Fire & EMS Storage

GearGrid offers storage systems for fire and EMS environments. These systems are commonly used to organize turnout gear, PPE, helmets, boots, duty bags, and other firehouse equipment.

Example product categories may include:
- Wall mount lockers
- Freestanding lockers
- Mobile lockers
- Two-tier lockers
- Benches
- Shelving and accessory storage
- Storage systems for damp or frequently used gear

## Athletic & Education Storage

GearGrid offers storage systems for schools, colleges, athletic departments, training facilities, and locker rooms. These systems are commonly used to organize team gear, uniforms, helmets, pads, balls, training equipment, and shared equipment.

Example product categories may include:
- Athletic lockers
- Mobile athletic storage
- Freestanding lockers
- Wall mount lockers
- Gear carts
- Shelving
- Workbenches
- Team storage systems

## Public Works Storage

GearGrid offers storage systems for public works and municipal facilities. These systems are commonly used to store PPE, uniforms, equipment, and tools used by crews.

Example product categories may include:
- PPE lockers
- Mobile lockers
- Wall-mounted storage
- Benches
- Shelving and accessory storage
- Storage systems for departments with changing layouts or limited wall space

## Tactical Storage

GearGrid offers storage systems for tactical and law enforcement-style facilities. These systems are commonly used to organize duty gear, tactical equipment, PPE, uniforms, and related accessories.

Example product categories may include:
- Tactical lockers
- Mobile or freestanding lockers
- Wall-mounted storage
- Stow-away seating
- Shelving and accessory storage

## Positioning Language

Preferred:
- “manufactures”
- “offers”
- “provides”
- “is designed for”
- “can support”
- “commonly used for”
- “availability and specifications vary by product and configuration”

Avoid:
- “guarantees”
- “eliminates”
- “only solution”
- “best in every situation”
- “available in every configuration”
