# Terminology — Gear Storage & Facility Storage

## Gear Storage

Gear storage refers to systems used to organize, protect, dry, display, or make equipment accessible in a facility.

## Equipment Storage

Equipment storage is a broad term for storing tools, PPE, uniforms, athletic gear, emergency response equipment, and other operational items.

## Turnout Gear Storage

Turnout gear storage refers to storage systems for firefighter protective gear such as coats, pants, helmets, boots, gloves, hoods, and related PPE.

## PPE Storage

PPE storage refers to storage for personal protective equipment used by fire, EMS, public works, tactical, industrial, and similar teams.

## Athletic Equipment Storage

Athletic equipment storage refers to storage systems for team gear, uniforms, helmets, pads, balls, training equipment, and locker room accessories.

## Wall Mount Locker

A wall mount locker is a locker system mounted to or supported along a wall to save floor space and organize gear.

## Freestanding Locker

A freestanding locker is a locker system designed to stand with floor support rather than depending primarily on wall mounting.

## Mobile Locker

A mobile locker is a locker or storage system designed to move, often using casters, so the storage layout can be adjusted.

## Open Locker

An open locker is a locker without a full door, often used where visibility, airflow, and quick access are important.

## Secure Locker

A secure locker includes features intended to restrict access or protect stored items. Exact security features vary by product and configuration.

## Gear Cart

A gear cart is a mobile storage system used to organize and transport equipment within or between facility areas.

## Locker Room Storage Planning

Locker room storage planning is the process of matching equipment, space, user needs, access patterns, ventilation, and workflow to an appropriate storage layout.
