# Product Category Facts — GearGrid

## Locker Systems

GearGrid locker systems are commonly used to store gear and equipment in fire, EMS, athletic, public works, and tactical environments. Locker systems may be wall-mounted, freestanding, mobile, or configured for specific equipment and facility needs.

## Wall Mount Lockers

Wall mount lockers are designed to use available wall space and help keep gear organized and off the floor. They are often considered when a facility wants dedicated storage while preserving open floor space.

## Freestanding Lockers

Freestanding lockers are designed for placement flexibility without relying solely on wall-mounted configurations. They may be considered when a room layout requires storage away from a wall or when facilities need modular layouts.

## Mobile Lockers

Mobile lockers are designed for facilities that need storage that can move or adapt as layouts change. They are commonly associated with multi-use spaces, shared equipment areas, and facilities where cleaning or reconfiguration matters.

## Bench Systems

Bench systems provide seating near lockers or equipment storage areas. In fire, athletic, public works, and tactical settings, bench systems can support dressing, gear changes, and equipment staging.

## Shelving and Accessories

Shelving and accessories help organize smaller equipment, bags, helmets, tools, and related gear. Accessory storage should be selected based on the equipment being stored, access needs, and available space.

## Open-Grid / Breathable Storage

Open-grid storage is commonly discussed as a way to support airflow around damp or frequently used gear. This can be relevant for turnout gear, athletic gear, PPE, and equipment that benefits from drying access and visibility.
